Our Founders

Brought together by a passion for live music and great coffee, our founders launched Cafe Sebastian in 2022.

Our Mission

We are passionate about bringing sublime musical experiences to new audiences in our beautiful cafe venue.

Our Artists

We are home to emerging artists and new voices, providing support, mentoring and performance opportunities. We are committed to fostering the arts through an all-year round programme of events, workshops and classes.

Our Community

We are a local, independent coffee house based in the heart of Exeter. Our coffee is good, our food is fresh and our vibe is friendly. We welcome patrons from all walks of life and look forward to serving you soon!
